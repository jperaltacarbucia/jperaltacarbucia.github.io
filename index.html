About Me

My name is Joshua Peralta. I was born in Puerto Rico, March 23 1994, and lived there for 12 years, although my family has been there since 1984. When I started school, it started at home with the home-schooling program and my mother as teacher. I've been home-schooled my whole life up until college. In 2006 we moved to Orlando, Florida and have been living there ever since. I was still home-schooled, but in high school I joined the online FLVS and then Connexus home-schooling programs.

In March 22, 2009 I got baptized and became a Christian. When I finished High School at 18, I went to college in Valencia. That was my first experience of taking classes out of my house by myself. I've never been the type of person to socialize, since I'm more of a nerd, you can always or mostly find me in the computer or on my gaming consoles, so this was quite something new. I did alright on the first semester, getting myself up in the Dean's Honor Students. Now I have graduated in 2017 with an AS: Digital Media Web Developer Degree!

Pass/Fail Test

For my last semester in college, I was told to do a Pass/Fail Test, which pretty much determined if I was going to graduate or not. I only had 24 hours to do the test, technicaly 1 whole day only to finish it. I was given a few text and images for resources and a few guidelines, after that it was all me to use my almost 6 years of experience to determined if I deserved to graduate as a web developer.

I began at exactly 12:00am and spent the first 4 hours coding a base and learning something new that I had never learned in my years at college, BUT it was required for this test. After the 4 hours I took a 7-8 hour sleep. Then spent 4 more hours coding and FINISHED! The website was finished with a mere 8 hours. I turned it in and passed! While Flash is not used anymore, it was still part of the test. So the top banner might work for some while it might not for others. (It doesn't work for me anymore)
