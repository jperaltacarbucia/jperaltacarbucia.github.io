What is the Pokemon Adoption Center?

The Pokemon Adoption Center, or PAC for short, is where you come and request breeded Pokemon with specific details for the Pokemon Sun and Moon games on the Nintendo 3DS.

You can claim these Pokemons during the next live stream on Twitch or even through Facebook!

Adopt a Pokemon

Fill out the form to request a Pokemon to adopt!
