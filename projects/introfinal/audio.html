|| ~ AUDIO PROJECT ~ ||

For the Audio Project, we have to record and make our own comercial. This project takes at least from 30 seconds to 1 minute and we are supposed to compose it with a message, background music, and sound effects. My comercial audio project was about Valencia and the class that I'm taking, which is Intro to Digital Media and Design. I explained 3 of the most important programs that you get to learn in the class, along with some interesting sounds to give you the idea.
